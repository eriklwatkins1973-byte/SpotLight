# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional community for everyone contributing to Spotlight.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions, reviews, and issue reports.
- Assume good intent and focus feedback on code, product, and process.
- Share context clearly when reporting bugs, vulnerabilities, or regressions.
- Support a collaborative environment across experience levels and backgrounds.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or hateful language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Disruptive trolling, bad-faith review behavior, or intimidation.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.

If behavior violates this Code of Conduct, maintainers may take action including warnings, temporary restrictions, or removal from collaboration channels.

## Reporting

To report conduct issues, contact project maintainers privately through repository channels.

Please include:

- What happened
- Where/when it occurred
- Any relevant links or screenshots

All reports are reviewed in good faith and handled with discretion.
